About Us


We're a team of 3 people that have been working as muscle therapists for several years!
Our goal is to make you healthy as soon as possible and give you the care that you need when you need it.

In addition to back- and neckpain, we can also help you fighting headaches, nausea, pain in shoulder and arms, hippain, kne and footproblems. If needed our muscle therapists also do paintreatment with needles.

We have a wave machine in our office, which has shown to be effective against inflammation in muscles and tendons, for example against tendinopathy like tenniselbow, achilles heel problems or plantar fascitt.

A longterm result and a good life quality is most important for us and therefore we strive to keep as updated as possible in our field of expertise. This in order to be able to provide the best possible care for our patients.
We also have a good cooporation with a doctorate specialist if the need arises.

Our clinic was build on communication, joy, trust and knowledge which you surely will notice while beeing one of our patients.
